Values of the League

Human impact

We care about the impact of data and algorithms on society. We believe that these tools can unlock an amazing amount of human good when built and used properly. We also understand that careless, malicious, and inequitable data systems have the potential to cause harm on a very large scale. We acknowledge that—like any product or decision making process—algorithms often face tradeoffs among competing human values, so the answers aren't always clear-cut and easy.

Technical understanding

We believe that engineering ethically sound algorithms requires a deep understanding of modern data systems. Good technical work is necessary (but not sufficient) for building Good algorithms. You can’t improve what you don’t understand.

Big tent

The Ethical Algorithms League isn't focused on any single issue. As a group, we don't endorse any specific policy or platform. We are a community that aids people in drawing their own, well-informed conclusions.

Bias to action

Once our members draw those conclusions, they take action. We believe that we can have a real impact on these issues, today. We seek out and share those opportunities.
