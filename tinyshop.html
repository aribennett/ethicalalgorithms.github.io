Overshoot

Created for Dave Beach to demonstrate casting fundementals in a fun way

The KED is an orthotic device built for Max Conserva to increase the extension force of his right knee. When he was 8 years old, Max was involved in a life changing accident that greatly damaged the quadricepts on his right knee, leaving them unable to bear weight without pain. I was the principal designer, with suport from Marshall Haltom in design and logistics. Eddie Momanyi and Jesse Shapiro provided testing support, and it was based on inital groundwork by Marshall Haltom and Susan Nitta
